My road to finally become a programmer was a long one. I always had a problem of having too many interests. I studied Psychology, Food Technology and Electronics and did not finish any of them, searching for the domain to which I will want to commit for the long haul. In the meantime, I held various odd jobs. I worked in a construction, paper carton production, electronics production, on a peony plantation, as a postman, bodyguard and a warehouseman. Being interested in subjects as different as philosophy, history, politics, weightlifting, dietetics, engineering and hard sciences I had to try various things to find what fits my character and abilities best. In 2017, after year of studying Electronics at the Lodz University of Technology I got a programming internship at Inovatica in Lodz. Already before that opportunity I developed fondness for programming. I did well there, so I took the risk, negotiated and got employed at the end of the internship. I dropped out of university and created rigorous plan of learning, which I had been executing religiously since then.

From September 2017 I have been working as a programmer at Inovatica in Lodz. At the very begining I was only developing native Android applications. Not long after that I was using Spring and Hibernate for creating backend applications. There was a period in which I was working on very customized and domain specific Java applications that interacts with ROS. Then again I worked on mobile apps, using Kotlin this time. At the moment I am back in the autonomous forklift project, which is being done almost absolutely in Java.

Programming is my greatest passion, so I am always learning something related to it and working on various projects. Documenting them is the main goal of this website. I consider myself a programmer, not only web, desktop or mobile one. Most of my experience is related to mobile and web development using Java and JavaScript related technologies. Nevertheless I am constantly expanding my expertise and will be happy to work in every domain related to programming applying the most suitable technology.
